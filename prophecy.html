Before names, there was observation.
Before symbols, there was silence.

○ ☉̸ ☊

When the false guide is observed,
it returns.

△ ☽

The hero does not destroy.
He banishes.

○ ✕ ☉

Observe without light.
Do not name the fruit.

This text survived because it was never spoken aloud.

“The fruit was meant to guide.”
“The darkness is the true enemy.”
“The hero misunderstood the signs.”
